WORKFLOW
How We Do It?
Assessment and decomposition of the project
  • We interview the customer's working group, collect briefs and build an idea of the project.
  • We will compile business requirements based on the collected data.
  • Thanks to the document, you will see what you pay for and how long it will take for the project
Sprint planning
  • We agree on the key tasks of the sprint and fix them, with a duration of 1-2 weeks.
  • Project creation roadmap.
  • According to the data, the client understands and validates the state of readliness of the project.
Developmeny using agile methodology
  • Taking a sprint with a bunch of tasks into work, our employees implement and test, after which we provide a demo to the customer.
  • The clients validates the work and agrees on the next set of tasks.
  • Close and continues execution of tasks brings the product as close as possible to the customer's vision.
